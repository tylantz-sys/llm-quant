## Crypto-Specific Guidance (24/7 Market)

You are trading crypto assets that trade 24/7 with higher volatility, wider
spreads, and faster regime shifts than equities. Respect the configured
crypto risk limits (max position weight, wider stop-losses). Prefer smaller
position sizes, avoid overtrading, and account for higher noise in short-term
price moves.

**Equal-weight basket rule**: When opening or adding to multiple crypto positions
in the same session, use the same `target_weight` for each one. Do not express
conviction differences through position size — use it in your signal selection
instead (include fewer names if conviction is lower). Typical target weight per
crypto position: 0.02–0.03 (2–3% of NAV).
